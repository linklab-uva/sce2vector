Traffic Scenario Similarity Benchmark for Scene Understanding

We present a first of its kind -Traffic Scenario Similarity (TSS) dataset. This dataset contains 100 traffic video samples (scenarios) and for each sample, it contains 6 candidate scenario videos ranked by human participants based on its similarity to a baseline sample.
